Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our bakery, products, orders, and delivery.

Orders & Products
Yes — our pastry studio specialises in custom celebration and wedding cakes. We handle everything from sketch to delivery. Orders need at least 2 weeks' notice; for wedding cakes we recommend 6–8 weeks. Send us a message with your date and vision and we'll arrange a tasting consultation.
Everything is baked fresh daily in our Baker Street kitchen starting at 4am. Bread and pastries have a 1–2 day shelf life; cakes and tarts keep for 3–5 days when refrigerated. We include best-before guidance on every order.
Standard orders can be modified or cancelled up to 12 hours before your chosen delivery or collection date. Custom cake orders must be cancelled at least 5 days in advance. Email hello@stackly.com with your order number and we'll assist promptly.
We love corporate work. We offer weekly bread baskets, breakfast catering, event pastry platters and branded packaging for corporate gifting. Minimum orders apply. Contact hello@stackly.com to discuss volumes and pricing.
Delivery & Collection
Yes. We ship to all mainland UK addresses Tuesday–Friday via next-day refrigerated courier. Orders must be placed before 12pm for same-day dispatch. Free delivery on orders over £50. Northern Ireland, Scottish Highlands and Channel Islands may incur a surcharge.
Absolutely. Choose "Click & Collect" at checkout and select your preferred location (Baker Street or Shoreditch). You'll receive an email when your order is ready — usually within 2 hours for standard items, 24 hours for custom orders.
Our refrigerated courier delivers between 7am and 7pm on your chosen day. You'll receive a tracking link by email once your order is dispatched. If nobody is home, the driver will leave your order in a safe cool location and notify you.
Allergens & Dietary
All our products are made in a kitchen that handles gluten, dairy, eggs, tree nuts and sesame. Each product listing on our site shows allergen tags (nut-free, vegan, GF etc.). Visit our Allergen Info page for full details.
Yes — we have a growing range of vegan and gluten-free products. Look for the "Vegan" and "GF" tags on product listings. Our vegan range is baked on dedicated trays but in a shared kitchen environment, so cross-contamination cannot be fully eliminated.
Returns & Wholesale
Because our products are fresh food, we can't accept returns on standard items. If something arrives damaged or below our quality standards, photograph it and email us within 24 hours of delivery. We'll arrange a full replacement or refund promptly.
Yes — we supply a small number of restaurants, cafés and delis across London. Trade accounts require a minimum weekly order and a signed agreement. Contact us with details about your business and our wholesale team will follow up within 2 business days.

Still have questions?

Our team is happy to help — reach out and we'll get back to you within one business day.
