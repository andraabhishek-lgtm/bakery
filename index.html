Our Story

Baking With Passion Since 2012

What started as a small weekend project in our grandmother's kitchen has grown into a beloved neighbourhood bakery.

We believe in the old way of doing things — slow fermentation, real butter, seasonal ingredients from local farms, and a genuine love for the craft. No shortcuts, no compromises.
