Returns & Refunds

Your satisfaction is our priority. If something isn't right, we'll make it right — quickly.

Our Policy at a Glance
🍞

Fresh Food Policy

Because our products are perishable fresh food, we cannot accept returns on standard orders. All sales are final once dispatched.

✅

Quality Guarantee

If your order arrives damaged, incorrect, or below our quality standards, we will offer a full replacement or refund, no questions asked.

⏱️

Report Within 24 Hours

All quality claims must be raised within 24 hours of delivery. Please keep the packaging and photograph any damaged items before contacting us.

💳

Refund Timeline

Approved refunds are processed within 3–5 business days and returned to your original payment method.

How to Raise a Claim
1

Photograph the issue

Take clear photos of the damaged or incorrect item(s) and the outer packaging before discarding anything.

2

Email us within 24 hours

Send your photos and order number to hello@stackly.com with the subject line "Quality Claim — Order #XXXX". Include a brief description of the problem.

3

We review and respond

Our customer care team will review your claim within 1 business day and confirm whether a replacement or refund will be issued.

4

Replacement or refund issued

Replacements are dispatched on the next available delivery day. Refunds are credited to your original payment method within 3–5 business days.

Order Cancellations
Standard orders can be cancelled up to 12 hours before the dispatch date for a full refund.
Custom cake orders can be cancelled up to 5 days before the collection or delivery date. Cancellations within 5 days will incur a 50% charge to cover materials already purchased and preparation begun.
To cancel: Email hello@stackly.com with your order number as soon as possible. We process cancellations during business hours (Mon–Fri, 8am–5pm).
