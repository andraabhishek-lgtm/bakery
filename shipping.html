Order & Dispatch Times
Cut-off time: Orders placed before 12pm Monday–Thursday are dispatched the same day. Orders placed after 12pm or on Friday are dispatched the next working day. We do not dispatch on Saturdays, Sundays, or bank holidays.
Delivery days: We deliver Tuesday through Friday only. This ensures your order arrives fresh and never sits over a weekend in transit.
Packaging: All orders are packed in insulated boxes with food-safe ice packs rated to keep contents below 8°C for up to 24 hours.
Tracking Your Order

Once your order is dispatched you'll receive an email with a courier tracking link. Most couriers provide a 1-hour delivery window on the morning of delivery. If you're not home, the driver will leave your order in a safe, shaded location and photograph it for our records.

Questions about your delivery? Email hello@stackly.com with your order number and we'll liaise with the courier on your behalf.
