Allergen Information

We take allergen safety seriously. Here's full transparency on what's in our kitchen and our products.

⚠️

Important: All Stackly products are made in a shared kitchen that handles gluten (wheat, barley, rye), dairy, eggs, tree nuts (including almonds, hazelnuts, walnuts, cashews), peanuts, sesame, and soya. If you have a severe allergy or anaphylactic reaction risk, please contact us directly before ordering so we can advise on suitability.
