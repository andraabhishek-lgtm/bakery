Frequently Asked Questions

All our products are made in a kitchen that handles gluten, dairy, eggs, tree nuts and sesame. Each product listing on our site shows allergen tags (nut-free, vegan, GF etc.). If you have a severe allergy, please email us before ordering so we can advise on suitability and cross-contamination risk.

Northern Ireland, Scottish Highlands and Channel Islands may incur a surcharge — see our Shipping Info page for full details.
